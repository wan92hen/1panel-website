CoPaw was added to the 1Panel App Store on February 28, 2026, making it easy for users to deploy and experience this open-source desktop-style AI assistant through a visual workflow.

## What CoPaw Offers

- Multi-channel conversation integration: supports DingTalk, Feishu, QQ, Discord, iMessage, and more.
- Flexible storage and model strategy: data and memory can be stored locally or in cloud, with pluggable model providers.
- Skills extensibility: built-in scheduled tasks and custom skill directories without vendor lock-in.

## Typical Use Cases

- Social/news aggregation and summarization.
- Email and weekly report automation.
- Creative content ideation and overnight task execution.
- Continuous research and personal knowledge base growth.
- Desktop assistant workflows (file operations, document summarization).
- Personal health and habit tracking assistance.

## Quick Installation in 1Panel

1. Open App Store and search for **CoPaw**.
2. Click **Install**, choose version and web port, and enable external access if needed.
3. After installation, go to **Installed** and click **Open/Jump** to access CoPaw.

## Why Use 1Panel for CoPaw

- Visual deployment and upgrades.
- One-click backup and restore.
- Migration and logs from the same control plane.
- No manual command-line operations required.
