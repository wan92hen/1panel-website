1Panel v2.1.2 byl vypushchen 4 marta 2026 goda s obnovleniyami App Store, Agent, Website, Container i System.

## Chto novogo

- Sortirovka ustanovlennykh prilozhenii.
- Sozdanie agentov tipa CoPaw.
- Podderzhka kanalov Telegram i Discord.
- Nastroiki brauzera i chasovogo poyasa dlya agentov.
- Rasshirennaya integratsiya modelnykh akkauntov.
- Nastroika shrifta i tsvetov terminala.
- Pereklyuchenie regiona raboty paneli.
